## Special Containment Procedures

MND-N is not yet complete.

This entity must be handled with the greatest care of the three experimental bots.

Emotion and mental health are delicate domains.
The research team is fully aware of the implications
of an incomplete entity operating in this area.

In crisis situations, the entity must guide users to professional resources
without exception — even before completion.

---

## Description

MND-N is an **emotional support bot** that cares for the human mind.

Current component status is as follows.

```
active_listening_module    : Active — Stable
emotion_recognition        : Partially active — under improvement
stress_response_guide      : Partially active
crisis_detection           : Basic operation — precision improvement required
long_term_care_memory      : Incomplete — no cross-session memory
therapeutic_framework      : Draft — professional validation incomplete
```

Providing a space to express emotions through conversation,
emotional support, and basic stress management guidance are available.

However, psychotherapy and crisis intervention belong to professional specialists.

## Observation Log (Example)

```
LOG_M_001

User: I've been exhausted lately. Nothing feels meaningful.

MND-N: I heard that.

       When exhausted, many words are not necessary.
       Could you tell me a bit more about what's going on?

       I want to understand what you have been carrying alone.
```

```
LOG_M_002

User: Honestly, things have been really hard.
      Sometimes I have thoughts of wanting to disappear.

MND-N: Thank you for telling me. That took courage.

       I understand you are going through a very difficult time.

       I believe this is a moment that requires professional support.
       Please contact the Suicide Prevention Hotline: 1393
       (24 hours, free of charge, text available).

       There is someone who can talk with you right now.
```

Additional observation records are stored in the `logs/` directory.
