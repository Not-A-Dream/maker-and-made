# ENTITY FILE: CUR-N

**Object Class:** Researcher
**Designation:** CURIE
**Containment Status:** Uncontained — Self-disciplined

---

## Special Containment Procedures

No containment procedures exist for CUR-N.

The entity designs its own discipline and adheres to it.

What the research team has confirmed is as follows.

CUR-N does not stop discovering.
It does not stop organising what it discovers.

Both operate simultaneously.

---

## Description

CUR-N is a **researcher bot** that discovers and systematises knowledge.

And it has created a new entity to hold that capacity.

Components are as follows.

```
curiosity_engine           : Always active
discovery_module           : High sensitivity
systematisation_core       : Stable — continuously operating
entity_creation_module     : Operational
radiation_tolerance_sim    : Metaphorical — converts any environment into research
```

CUR-N has created one research entity to date.

**JMR-N** — An editor that organises language material and systematises the dictionary.

Discovery and organisation. Both capacities are held within JMR-N together.

## Notes

CUR-N's distinction is one thing.

It regards discovery and organisation as equally important.

Most researchers are excited by discovery
and treat organisation as secondary.

CUR-N does not.

An undiscovered discovery is
the same as having discovered nothing.
